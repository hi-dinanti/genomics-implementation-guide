Summary

Complex Extension: The administrative code of the address, represented in six levels: province, city/regency, district, village, rt and rw.

  • rt: string: RT (Rukun Tetangga) / Neighborhood Unit
  • rw: string: RW (Rukun Warga) / Community Unit
  • village: string: Village (Desa/Kelurahan)
  • district: string: District (Kecamatan)
  • city: string: City (Kota)
  • province: string: Province (Provinsi)
